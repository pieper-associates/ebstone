Azalea, Camellia & Acid Mix

Highlights

A rich, acidic blend specifically formulated for azaleas, camellias, rhododendrons, gardenias, and other acid-loving plants. Contains aged forest products, sphagnum peat moss, and composted fir bark.

Description

EB Stone Azalea, Camellia & Acid Mix provides the ideal growing environment for acid-loving plants. The blend is designed to retain moisture while maintaining proper drainage, with a lower pH that helps acid-loving varieties absorb the nutrients they need to thrive.

Additional Details

Use as a planting mix or soil amendment. Mix thoroughly with native soil when planting in the ground, or use straight in containers. For best results, apply a top dressing annually.
